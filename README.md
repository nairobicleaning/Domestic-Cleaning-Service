## Importance of Domestic Cleaning

A clean home contributes to both physical health and mental well-being. Regular **Domestic Cleaning in Nairobi** helps to:

* Eliminate dust, allergens, and bacteria
* Improve indoor air quality
* Prevent pest infestations
* Enhance comfort and organization
* Promote a healthier lifestyle

Neglecting household cleaning can lead to accumulation of dirt, mold, and germs that may pose health risks.

## Core Domestic Cleaning Services

### 1. General House Cleaning

This includes dusting, sweeping, mopping, and organizing living spaces. It forms the foundation of **Domestic Cleaning in Nairobi** services.

### 2. Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning targets hidden dirt, grime, and bacteria in hard-to-reach areas such as under furniture, behind appliances, and inside cabinets.

### 3. Kitchen Cleaning

Kitchen cleaning involves degreasing surfaces, cleaning appliances, and sanitizing countertops and sinks to maintain hygiene standards.

### 4. Bathroom Cleaning

Bathrooms require thorough disinfection to remove stains, mold, and bacteria. This is a critical aspect of **Domestic Cleaning in Nairobi**.

### 5. Laundry and Ironing

Some services include washing, drying, folding, and ironing clothes for convenience.

### 6. Move-In and Move-Out Cleaning

This service ensures homes are thoroughly cleaned before occupancy or after relocation.

### 7. Post-Construction Cleaning

After renovations, homes often require specialized cleaning to remove dust, debris, and paint stains.

These services ensure comprehensive **Domestic Cleaning in Nairobi** for all types of households.
